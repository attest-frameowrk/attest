Aethon Robotics: Company Overview

Aethon Robotics is a fictional autonomous delivery company founded in 2024.
The company specializes in last-mile delivery using autonomous ground vehicles
called AethonBots. Each AethonBot is equipped with LiDAR, stereo cameras, and
a custom path-planning processor called the NavCore 3.

AethonBots operate in urban environments and can carry payloads up to 25 kg.
They navigate sidewalks and bike lanes using a combination of pre-mapped routes
and real-time obstacle avoidance. The fleet management platform, AethonFleet,
coordinates hundreds of bots simultaneously using a centralized dispatch engine.

Key metrics:
- Average delivery time: 18 minutes within a 3 km radius
- Delivery success rate: 99.2%
- Fleet size: 450 bots across 12 cities
- Battery life: 8 hours per charge cycle
